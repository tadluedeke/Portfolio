About Tad

I've worked in multiple industries, and have held a wide variety of roles in my career so far.
From my summer jobs working in cemeteries to marketing, sales, small-business needs, purchasing and inventory management, and overall operations, I've been there, done that, and can give insight on many areas of any organization.

I enjoy projects with a lot of detail where I can see the overall goal, and work with others to achieve that goal. This led me to take the Software Developent course through Eleven Fifty Academy in the hopes that my love for creating complex Excel spreadsheets would turn into a new career with exciting opportunities.

When I'm not at work, I enjoy playing board games with my neighbors and painting scale models.
